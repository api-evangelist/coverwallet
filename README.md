# CoverWallet

CoverWallet, an Aon company, is a digital insurance marketplace with a REST API for quoting, binding, and managing small business commercial insurance policies across multiple carriers. The platform supports general liability, business owners policy (BOP), professional liability, workers compensation, commercial property, and other commercial lines.

Partners can integrate real-time quoting, underwriting, binding, billing, and servicing capabilities directly into their applications. Target integration partners include lenders, payroll providers, accounting software vendors, real estate platforms, and agency management systems.

## Carriers Supported

Liberty Mutual, CNA, Starr, Chubb, Blackboard (AIG), Guard, Markel, Employers, Travelers, Progressive, Hiscox, and Atlas.
